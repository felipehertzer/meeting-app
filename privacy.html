Privacy Policy

Your conversations should stay under your control.

Badger.ai is designed for local recording, transcription, notes, playback, and optional iCloud storage using the user’s Apple account.

Last updated: 14 May 2026

Summary

Badger.ai does not sell personal data, does not show ads, and does not use third-party tracking in the current release.

Data We Handle Audio and Transcripts iCloud Calendar Notifications Apple Intelligence Sharing Retention Contact

Data We Handle

Badger.ai may handle audio recordings, generated transcripts, speaker labels, summaries, notes, action items, highlights, comments, calendar event titles, notification preferences, and app settings.

The app uses local storage for conversation data and UserDefaults for lightweight preferences such as notification settings.

Audio and Transcripts

Audio recordings are created when you start recording. Transcripts are generated from speech recognition and attached to the conversation. You can edit transcript text and speaker names inside the app.

Badger.ai does not operate its own server for uploaded audio or transcript storage in the current release.

iCloud Storage

If iCloud is available, Badger.ai may copy audio recordings to the app’s iCloud container so they can be stored with your Apple account. If iCloud is unavailable, recordings remain saved locally on the device.

iCloud storage is governed by Apple’s iCloud terms and settings. You can manage iCloud Drive availability in iOS Settings.

Calendar Access

Calendar access is optional. If you grant access, Badger.ai reads upcoming events so they can appear in the Calendar tab and so you can start a conversation from an event title.

Badger.ai does not edit, create, or delete calendar events in the current release.

Notifications and Preferences

Badger.ai uses notification preferences to decide whether to show app activity such as recording completion, iCloud sync status, and action item reminders. These preferences are stored on the device.

Apple Intelligence and Speech Recognition

When supported by the device and operating system, Badger.ai uses Apple platform capabilities for speech recognition and generated notes. Availability and processing behavior may depend on the iOS version, device, language, and Apple account settings.

If future versions add a separate cloud service, this policy will be updated before that service is used.

Sharing and Third Parties

Badger.ai does not sell personal data and does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs in the current release. Content is shared only when you use iOS sharing features, copy content, export a transcript, or otherwise choose to send it outside the app.

Retention and Deletion

Conversations remain available until you delete them or remove the app’s data. Deleting a conversation removes its transcript, notes, comments, and local audio file. iCloud copies may also be removed when available to the app.
