Recording and Live Transcription

Tap the microphone button to start a new conversation. Notter.ai begins recording audio and displays live transcript text as speech is recognized. Tap the stop control to end the recording and save the conversation.

For best results, keep the device nearby, avoid covering the microphone, and confirm microphone permission is enabled in iOS Settings.

Saved Audio Playback

When a conversation has an audio file, the playback dock appears in the conversation detail screen. Use play or pause, skip controls, the timeline, and playback speed to review the meeting.

Audio is saved locally first. If iCloud is available for the app, Notter.ai attempts to sync the recording so it can remain available across compatible devices.

Calendar

The Calendar tab can show upcoming events after you grant calendar access. Tap the microphone button on an event to start a named conversation using that event title.

If events do not appear, open iOS Settings, confirm Calendar permission is enabled for Notter.ai, then return to the Calendar tab and refresh.

Editing Transcripts and Speakers

Open a conversation, select Transcript, then use the edit control on a transcript segment to update the text or speaker name. Use Rematch speakers from the conversation menu to rename speakers across the full transcript.

Highlights and comments stay connected to transcript segments when possible.

Notifications

The bell button shows recent app activity such as saved recordings, summaries, action items, comments, highlights, and storage status. Notification preferences are available from Account settings.

Delete or Archive a Conversation

Use the menu on a conversation card or the conversation detail menu to archive, restore, or delete a conversation. Deleting removes the transcript, notes, comments, and the local audio file for that conversation.

Troubleshooting

  • If recording does not start, check microphone permission and available storage.
  • If transcription is delayed, move closer to the speaker and wait a moment for speech recognition to finalize text.
  • If iCloud sync is unavailable, confirm iCloud Drive is enabled and that the device is signed into iCloud.
  • If playback controls do not appear, the selected conversation may not have a saved audio file.
